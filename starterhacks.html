About StarterHacks

StarterHacks is all about learning, and harbouring a culture of creation and collaboration. We love first time hackers and are focused on helping you achieve your goals and expand your portfolio. Join us and 250 of your peers for a weekend of hacking and ideation. Learn important skills in coding, design and pitching, and tinker with multiple APIs and hardware. So come together to learn, make friends, and create cool new projects.
